# One-Page-Company-Profile
**Project Overview**

This project presents a "One Page Profile" and deep-dive financial analysis of ITC Limited, a leading Indian diversified conglomerate. Prepared for academic purposes, this report evaluates the company's multi-segment operations, historical financial performance, and recent corporate restructurings as of April 2026.

**Key Features**

Segmental Analysis: Detailed breakdown of revenue contributions across Cigarettes (43%), FMCG (29%), Agri-Business (20%), Paper & Packaging (7%), and Hotels (1%).Financial Metrics (FY21–FY25): Analysis of Total Sales, EBITDA margins, and a significant jump in Net Profit Margin to 46.13% in March 2025.Valuation Ratios: Tracking of Price-to-Earnings (P/E), EV/EBITDA, and Return on Equity (ROE), which reached 50% in the latest fiscal year.Recent Corporate Updates: * Operational control of Yoga Bar (Sproutlife Foods) to bolster the FMCG portfolio.Completion of the ITC Hotels Ltd demerger.Analysis of stock pressure following cigarette excise duty hikes in February 2026.
